Kevin is a certified Biological Technical Assistant. He completed his BTA training in 2023 at the Lise-Meitner School in Berlin and then gained work experience at the University of La Laguna in Tenerife, where he supported various projects focused on developing new anticancer drugs. He then joined the Charité’s department of dental materials science and biomaterials research, contributing to work on biodegradable implant materials. Within Charité, he later transitioned to his current team. Alongside his work as a BTA, Kevin is pursuing a B.Sc. in Biology at Freie Universität Berlin and in his free time, he loves to travel and socialize with friends.
